Tính năng nổi bật

Tất cả feature tốt most từ Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana — cộng thêm AI and quantum-safety.

🔐

Quantum-Safe Native

Chữ ký CRYSTALS-Dilithium-3 (NIST FIPS 204) bảo vệ mọi giao dịch khỏi tấn công bằng máy tính lượng tử.

🧠

Proof of Useful Work

Validator chạy AI inference, training, scientific compute thay vì hash vô nghĩa. Mỗi watt điện đều có ích.

🤖

AI Agent Wallet

Dedicated wallet for AI: spending policy, intent-based tx, death-switch, reputation. Agents live autonomously on the blockchain.

⚡

x402 Micropayment

Trả phí per-API-call, per-token LLM. Payment channels off-chain settle 1 triệu micro-tx in 1 onchain tx.

🛒

AI Marketplace On-chain

Đăng ký model, dataset, compute lên chain. Royalty tự động, không need trung gian. Creators kiếm tiền từng inference.

🔥

Deflationary Burn

50% of tx fees + 30% of AI fees burned every block. The more adoption grows, the more supply shrinks. Net deflation from year ~7.

🛡️

EVM Compatible + AI Opcodes

Mọi smart contract Ethereum chạy được, cộng thêm 8 opcode AI native: LLM_INFER, ZK_VERIFY, INTENT_RESOLVE...

🏛️

Hybrid AI/Human Governance

Quadratic voting for holders + AI Council tư vấn + Human Council veto khẩn cấp. Governance không bị cá voi.

🌉

PQ-Secured Bridges

Cross-chain with Ethereum, Solana, Cosmos via quantum-encrypted bridge. Bring current assets into the future.

Code chạy in 30 giây

Prototype Python ~800 dòng — không dependency ngoài. Đọc, chạy, fork.

# 1. Tạo wallet quantum-safe from quantum_wallet import Wallet alice = Wallet.create(height=4) print(alice.address) # qta1c6a6bff372ce72e4902a1dc3008940a02f71c4 # 2. Tạo AI agent with spending policy from ai_agent import AIAgentWallet, SpendingPolicy policy = SpendingPolicy(max_per_tx=0.5, max_per_hour=5.0) bot = AIAgentWallet.create(owner_address=alice.address, name="ResearchBot-001", policy=policy, initial_funding=2.0) # 3. Open payment channel for 1000 micropayments from ai_agent import PaymentChannel channel = PaymentChannel(bot, "qta1openai_api", deposit=1.0) for _ in range(1000): channel.micro_pay(0.0001) # off-chain, gần như free channel.close() # settle on-chain with 1 tx
